We offer a range of resources aimed at helping teachers and parents better understand autism and neurodiversity. Our popular book, Say Hello to Hedgy, has contributed to the introduction of ‘Hedgy Club’, a program West Sussex County Council has set up as part of the ‘Autism for Schools’ Project’.

In addition, we provide resources on Developmental Language Disorder (DLD). For more information, please contact us.

We regularly visit schools and nurseries, to share insights into neurodiversity, discussing its impact on individuals and how we celebrate uniqueness. If you’d like us to visit your school and host an event for children, please reach out via our website.
